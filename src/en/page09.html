Svadhyaya

Study of Spiritual Texts

Svadhyaya (self-study) is the second niyama in the yoga system. It includes the study of spiritual literature, sacred texts, and reflection on their meaning.

Through svadhyaya, the practitioner deepens their understanding of spiritual principles and approaches the realization of their true nature. It is not just reading, but deep immersion in spiritual knowledge.

Knowledge liberates, ignorance binds
